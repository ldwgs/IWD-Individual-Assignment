    About Us

    UOW Malaysia KDU is a private university college in Malaysia, with its flagship campus in Utropolis Glenmarie, Shah Alam. Established in 1983, and a pioneer in Malaysian private education, UOW Malaysia KDU was one of the first private colleges in Malaysia to have its own purpose-built campus.

    Our Mission

    Molding the minds of the future of Malaysia's greatest and allowing them to flourish to the best of their respective potentials.

    Our Plan

    Encouraging students to not only better themselves, but to also allow them to find themselves within the journey towards their eternal goal.

    Our Vision

    Shaping the Malaysian youth into becoming world leaders. Breaking the mold of what is possible in education whilst retaining integrity.
